BlogForge — design auditions

Two directions for the long-form drafting workspace. Same workflow (single scrolling document, collapsible setup, outline minimap, continuous prose feel) — different skin.

Option A

Writer (quiet)

iA Writer / Ulysses energy. Paper, ink, one slate-blue accent. Source Serif body type. No chrome. Reads like a serious place to write.
Option B

Notebook (soft)

Notion / Linear energy. Soft gray app, white rounded cards, friendly cobalt accent, expandable section cards. Inter + Lora.
Both fix the workflow the same way: single scrolling document instead of three pages, setup hidden behind a disclosure at the top, sections rendered as a continuous piece (no separate "Sections" tab), and an outline minimap in the sidebar for navigation + status at a glance.
